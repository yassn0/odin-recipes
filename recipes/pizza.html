Pizza

Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat, round bread dough topped with tomato sauce and cheese. It is a popular dish in many parts of the world, including Italy, the United States, and Canada.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pizza cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pizza dough
  2. Add the pizza sauce
  3. Add the pizza cheese

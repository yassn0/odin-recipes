Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking. Either term can also refer to the pasta itself, a category of prepared dishes made from the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pasta sauce
  • Pasta cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Add the pasta sauce
  3. Add the pasta cheese
